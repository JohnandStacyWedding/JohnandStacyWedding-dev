The Bride and Groom

THE BEGINNING

In 2013, John and Stacy started graduate school at NC State University, where they met in a Bible study for graduate students. For several weeks, John found Stacy laying in the grass on campus and began scheduling his walks to coincide with her siestas. When Stacy's multicolor pen broke, she asked John to do some engineering magic. Although he was unable to fix it, he got an assorted set and gave her a replacement in her favorite color. Stacy was smitten. After long hours in the library, laughter, and shared beliefs and interests, the connection grew between them. With graduation, employment separated them geographically, but the roots of their relationship were already planted. The relationship continued to be cultivated through tens of thousands of miles of driving and many hours of phone calls. Now, it is blossoming into marriage.

THE ENGAGEMENT

John knew he wanted to propose in person, outside near a waterfall. (Stacy loves waterfalls.) Their trip to New Hampshire's White Mountains was the perfect opportunity. He proposed on a tree in front of the frozen Arethusa Falls.

THE WEDDING

The wedding will be held in September near Cedar Mountain, NC.
